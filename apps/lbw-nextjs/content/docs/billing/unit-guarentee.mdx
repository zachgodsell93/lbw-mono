title: 10 Unit Guarentee
description: Below we outline how the 10 unit guarentee is calculated and applied to your account
---

The 10 unit guarentee is available to users on our Pro Subscription. If you don't make 10 units profit in a given week after Betfair Comission you don't pay for your
next week's subscription cost

## How is the 10 unit guarentee calculated?

The 10 unit guarentee is calculated on a weekly basis that runs from Monday 5:00am AEST through to Sunday 11:00pm AEST. We total the number of units that you have
accumiliated after comission. This will be relative to the unit sizing on each bet, which is why we use units and not a dollar figure. This gives you the ability to increase
or reduce your stake size relative to your bank roll without affecting your unit guarentee.

## When is the unit guarentee applied?

Your account will be credited on Monday mornings between 5am-8am AEST.

If your subscription comes out on a Sunday or Saturday in particular, it is important to remember that since this is applied on a Monday that it will reflect
on the current weeks subscription.

## Does anything void my 10 unit guarentee

Yes, there are a couple of scenarios where your 10 unit guarentee would be voided.

The most likely scenarios are the following:

- You turn your betting automation off whilst races are still running and that causes you to miss an order being placed
- You set your Target profit amount below 7 units
- You set your stop loss amount higher than 4 units.

## What happens if my account is not credited for the 10 unit guarentee, where it should have.

If your 10 unit guarentee has not been applied and believe that it should have. Please feel free to contact us so that we can investigate.

Unfortunately, technology isn't always perfect and there may have been an incorrect error made. In which case we are more than happy to rectify.
